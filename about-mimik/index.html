who we are?

Miphawell unit's vision is to create a healthcare landscape where individuals worldwide have seamless access to personalized treatment plans. We aim to empower individuals to actively manage their health, leveraging the transformative power of Miphawell AI. By analyzing personal, medical, and lifestyle data, we pioneer proactive health management, leading to improved well-being and quality of life for all.

what Miphawell do?

Transform your mobile device into a reliable healthcare companion with Miphawell. Seamlessly integrating into your daily life, Miphawell empowers you with comprehensive medical assistance for diagnostics, prevention, guidance, and real-time treatment. It adapts and learns to facilitate self-care and proficiently monitor your daily medications, supplements, diet, and activity levels based on your symptoms, ensuring proactive management of health concerns.

Our Values

MiphaWell Smart AI transforms your mobile device into a personal medical assistant, offering a wide array of health services. From diagnostics to prevention, suggestions to real-time treatment, it provides a comprehensive suite of tools. By learning from user input, it enables effective self-management and streamlined monitoring of daily medications, supplements, nutrition, and activity levels, addressing health concerns with precision and efficiency

Miphawell users leverage Miphawell AI to connect and engage with healthcare professionals, pharmacies, as well as friends and family concerning medical issues or symptoms. They have the option to choose contacts such as friends, family members, doctors, and pharmacies for direct, real-time communication, enabling them to receive valuable suggestions and feedback from medical experts

Highest content management and security are ensured with Miphwell RS200 Communication (MRS20) from the physical layer up to the application layer. Additionally, Miphawell SD300 Communication extends from the Miphawell Protocols Adaptations Layer up to the Healthcare IoT application layer, further enhancing the comprehensive communication framework provided by Miphawell

Miphawell Healthcare ensures content security through its cloud monitoring capabilities, particularly focusing on its Drive Layer. This layer enables the implementation of Virtual Smart Select Session Transport Protocols (V3STP) from an architectural perspective. This robust framework enhances the security and efficiency of data transmission within the Miphawell platform. Applications leveraging the Miphawell platform benefit from advanced features such as Device Discovery and Peer-to-Peer (P2P) communication seamlessly integrated within V3STP. These functionalities not only optimize data exchange but also ensure secure and reliable connections between devices, crucial for healthcare applications where confidentiality and integrity are paramount.
